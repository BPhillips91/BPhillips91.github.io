For starters, my name is Brendan. I am the oldest boy out of 6 siblings.I love to have fun, and when I'm not at work or school, you'll probably find me playing video games. I have recently picked up country western dancing, and to my surprise, I have become a pretty good dancer! I have played soccer for 14 years.

I wasn't always the student that I currently am. I used to be a troublemaker and was constantly disrupting the classroom. If I wasn't causing chaos in the classroom, I was probably skipping class to get high. I never took my schooling seriously and eventually dropped out in what should have been my junior year of high school. After dropping out of high school, my drug use took a turn for the worse. I went from only smoking marijuana, to taking xanax, to using methamphetamines on a daily basis. This would eventually lead me to selling narcotics to support my habit. Thankfully, I was able to see what this type of lifestyle would lead to, and that was not the life that I wanted for myself. I was able to put down the meth cold turkey.

After sobering up, I began to work doing manual labor. At the time I felt the money was good, but boy does that Texas heat take the life out of you. I would work all day, get off, go home, shower, then go straight to bed, just to be up before sunrise to start the cycle over again. Then came a blessing in disguise, I was arrested for possession of marijuana. While I sat in a freezing jail cell, I was able to think about my life and what I was doing with it.. This was the impetus that I needed to get my behind back in school.
